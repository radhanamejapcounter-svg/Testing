HOW ONE-DEVICE WORKS:
----------------------
Phone A logs in → writes activeSession:"dev_A" to Firestore
Phone B logs in → writes activeSession:"dev_B" to Firestore  
Phone A's real-time listener sees activeSession:"dev_B" ≠ "dev_A"
Phone A immediately signs out + shows message
Result: only Phone B is active → NO duplicate jap counts!

WHY THIS FIXES SPURIOUS COUNTS:
---------------------------------
Before: Both devices were signed in simultaneously.
Both were tapping/listening → Firestore got double increments.
fbApplyRemote merged them → counts doubled.

After: Only ONE device can be signed in at a time.
The other gets evicted the moment a new device logs in.
Single source of truth = accurate counts. 🙏
